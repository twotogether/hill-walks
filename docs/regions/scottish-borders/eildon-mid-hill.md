## Description

Eildon Mid Hill is the highest of the three Eildon peaks, located in the Scottish Borders near Melrose. The distinctive triple-peaked hill is a prominent landmark visible for miles around. The walk offers excellent views across the Borders landscape.

## Route

Start from Melrose town centre and follow the marked path up the north slope. The terrain is steep in places but well-established. The summit offers panoramic views of the River Tweed valley and surrounding hills.
